## TS Dharani Portal: Telangana's Unified Land Records Platform

The Telangana State **Dharani portal** — launched in 2020 and significantly enhanced by 2026 — is the single integrated platform for all land records, registration, mutation, and encumbrance data in the state. It covers both agricultural and non-agricultural land and has eliminated manual processes for most land record transactions.

---

## Key Functions of TS Dharani in 2026

| Function | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Record of Rights (ROR / Pahani) | View and download — shows owner name, survey number, extent, land type, and rights |
| Pattadar Passbook | Digital passbook issued to registered landowner confirming ownership |
| Mutation (Transfer of Registration) | Online ownership transfer request after property purchase |
| Encumbrance Certificate | Shows all registered transactions and liabilities — critical for home loans |
| Market Value Certificate | Government-determined value for stamp duty calculation |

---

## How to Access Telangana Land Records — April 2026

1. Visit **dharani.telangana.gov.in**
2. Click on 'Search Agricultural Land Records' or 'View Non-Agricultural Properties'
3. Enter survey number, village, mandal, or Pattadar's Aadhaar number
4. View the ROR — download PDF for official submissions
5. For Encumbrance Certificate: use the **IGRS Telangana portal** (igrs.telangana.gov.in)

---

## Mutation Process in Telangana — 2026

After registering a property sale in Telangana, mutation (transfer of pattadar name in land records) must be done through the Dharani portal.

**The process:**
1. Submit online mutation request with the registered document number
2. Revenue Divisional Officer verifies
3. Mutation completed and updated Pattadar Passbook issued
4. **Timeline:** typically 30–45 days

## For Home Loan Applicants in Telangana

Lenders require verified, encumbrance-free title before disbursing home loans for Telangana properties:

1. Obtain an **Encumbrance Certificate for at least 13 years** from IGRS Telangana confirming no prior mortgages or disputes
2. Ensure the seller's name in the **Pattadar Passbook matches the sale deed** — any mismatch will hold up the loan
3. Verify RERA registration for under-construction properties
