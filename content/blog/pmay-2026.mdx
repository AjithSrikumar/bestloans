## PMAY in April 2026: What is Active

Two schemes are running in April 2026:
- **PMAY Urban 2.0 (PMAY-U 2.0)** — covering urban homebuyers with loans sanctioned on or after 1 September 2024, running through 2029
- **PMAY Gramin (PMAY-G)** — for rural households

PMAY-U 2.0 replaces the earlier CLSS structure with a revised **Interest Subsidy Scheme (ISS)**.

---

## PMAY-U 2.0 Subsidy Details — April 2026

| Criteria | Details |
|----------|---------|
| Who qualifies | EWS (annual income up to Rs. 3 lakh) and LIG (up to Rs. 9 lakh) |
| Eligible loan amount | Home loans up to **Rs. 25 lakh**, sanctioned from 1 Sept 2024 |
| Maximum subsidy | **Rs. 1,80,000** (NPV of 4% interest subsidy on first Rs. 8 lakh for 12 years) |
| Minimum loan tenure | 5 years |
| Women ownership | Mandatory — property must be in woman's name (sole or co-owner) for EWS/LIG |
| Previous benefit | Must not have received any central/state housing assistance in the last 20 years |

## The Old PMAY CLSS — Still Relevant for Existing Borrowers

For loans sanctioned before August 2024 under the original CLSS scheme:

| Category | Income | Subsidy Rate | Max Loan | Max Benefit |
|----------|--------|-------------|---------|-------------|
| EWS/LIG | Up to Rs. 6 lakh | 6.5% | Rs. 6 lakh | Rs. 2,67,000 |
| MIG I | Rs. 6–12 lakh | 4% | Rs. 9 lakh | Rs. 2,35,000 |
| MIG II | Rs. 12–18 lakh | 3% | Rs. 12 lakh | Rs. 2,30,000 |

If you were sanctioned before August 2024 and haven't received subsidy yet, check with your lender — the benefit may still be claimable.

## PMAY-G (Gramin) — Rural Update April 2026

PMAY-G provides financial assistance to rural households in kutcha/kaccha houses to construct permanent pucca homes:
- Minimum unit size: **25 sq. metres**
- Financial assistance transferred via Direct Benefit Transfer (DBT) to Aadhaar-linked bank accounts
- Selection based on Socio-Economic and Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data
- Phase III is ongoing — check beneficiary status at the Awaassoft portal

## How to Apply for PMAY in April 2026

**Online:**
1. Apply at pmaymis.gov.in → select 'Apply for PMAY-U 2.0'
2. Complete the application with required details

**Through your lender:**
Most banks and HFCs that are PLIs (Primary Lending Institutions) under the scheme can process the PMAY application at the time of loan sanction.

**Documents needed:**
- Aadhaar card
- Income certificate
- Bank details
- Property documents
- Loan sanction letter

**Track status:** at pmaymis.gov.in using Aadhaar number or application ID
