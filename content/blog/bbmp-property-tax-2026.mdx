## BBMP Property Tax 2026: Key Dates and Rebate Structure

The Bruhat Bengaluru Mahanagara Palike (BBMP) levies property tax annually on residential, commercial, and industrial properties within Bengaluru's limits. For FY 2026–27:

- Tax is payable from **1 April 2026**
- **5% rebate** available for taxpayers who pay in full before **30 April 2026**
- This makes April the financially optimal window for property owners

---

## How BBMP Property Tax is Calculated in 2026

BBMP uses the **SAS (Self Assessment Scheme)** method:

```
Tax = Zonal Classification × Built-up Area × Age Factor × Depreciation × Usage Factor × Occupancy Status
```

| Factor | Impact |
|--------|--------|
| Zonal classification (A to E) | Zone A areas (Koramangala) attract highest rates |
| Self-occupied residential | Lower rates than rented properties |
| Age of structure | Older buildings get depreciation benefit |

## How to Pay BBMP Property Tax Online — Step by Step

**Step 1:** Visit **bbmptax.karnataka.gov.in** (BBMP's official property tax portal)

**Step 2:** Click 'Pay Property Tax' → enter your SAS Application Number or Khata number

**Step 3:** Verify property details and the tax amount displayed

**Step 4:** Select payment mode — net banking, debit/credit card, or UPI

**Step 5:** Complete payment → download and save the payment receipt (PDF)

---

## How to Download Your BBMP Property Tax Receipt — 2026

1. Visit bbmptax.karnataka.gov.in → 'Receipts' section
2. Enter application number
3. Download the official challan/receipt

This receipt is valid as property tax paid proof for legal, home loan, and registration purposes.

---

## Why BBMP Property Tax Matters for Home Loan Applicants

Banks and HFCs require the latest BBMP property tax paid receipt as part of the **property due diligence documents** for home loans on Bengaluru properties. An unpaid property tax can:
- Create liabilities that complicate the title
- Prevent disbursement
- Delay sanction

> **Ensure the current and last 2–3 years' receipts are available** — lenders often request them during property verification.

---

## BBMP Khata Certificate and Khata Extract

Beyond property tax payment, the **BBMP Khata Certificate** (issued in the owner's name) and **Khata Extract** (showing property details) are critical documents for home loans and property registrations in Bengaluru.

Apply for Khata transfer (mutation) immediately after buying any property in the BBMP jurisdiction — it should show the new owner's name.
