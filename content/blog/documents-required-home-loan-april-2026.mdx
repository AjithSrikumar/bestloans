## Why Documentation is the Biggest Bottleneck

In April 2026, while banks have significantly digitised KYC, income verification, and property valuation, **documentation errors and mismatches remain the No. 1 cause of home loan delays**.

The most common issue: mismatches between Form 16, AIS (Annual Information Statement), and bank statements — lenders now cross-check all three systematically.

---

## Universal KYC Documents — All Borrowers

- **PAN card** — mandatory for all financial transactions above Rs. 50,000
- **Aadhaar card** — mandatory for identity and address verification; also used for video KYC (V-KYC)
- **Photographs** — 2–3 recent passport-size photographs
- **Address proof** — Aadhaar, utility bill (not older than 3 months), bank passbook, or registered rental agreement

---

## Income Documents — Salaried Borrowers

| Document | Requirement |
|----------|------------|
| Salary slips | Latest 3–6 months (all components shown) |
| Form 16 | Last 2 financial years (Part A + Part B) |
| Bank statements | Last 6 months showing salary credits |
| ITR acknowledgment | Last 2–3 years |
| Employment proof | Appointment letter if in current job less than 1 year |
| AIS | Annual Information Statement — lenders cross-check against Form 16 |

---

## Income Documents — Self-Employed Borrowers

| Document | Requirement |
|----------|------------|
| ITR with computation | Last 3 years (e-filed, acknowledged) |
| Balance Sheet + P&L | Last 3 years (CA-audited) |
| Business continuity proof | GST registration + returns, Trade Licence, or Partnership Deed |
| Business bank statements | Last 12 months (primary operating account) |
| Tax Audit Report | If turnover exceeds Rs. 1 crore or receipts exceed Rs. 25 lakh |

---

## Property Documents — All Borrowers

- Agreement to Sell or Sale Deed (draft or executed)
- Title deed and chain of property title documents (last 15–30 years)
- **RERA registration certificate** — mandatory for under-construction projects in 2026
- Approved building plan from the local municipal/development authority
- Encumbrance Certificate (EC) — confirms no existing mortgage or liabilities
- Latest property tax receipts
- Occupancy Certificate (OC) — for completed/ready properties
- NOC from builder/housing society — for resale or society flats

---

## NRI-Specific Documents — April 2026

- Valid Indian passport with current residence visa page
- OCI/PIO card (if applicable)
- Employment contract and salary certificate from foreign employer (in English)
- Foreign bank statements: last 6 months (salary credits in foreign currency)
- NRE/NRO account statements in India: last 6 months
- Power of Attorney (notarised and apostilled) — if borrower cannot be present in India
- ITR filed in country of residence

---

## Common Documentation Mistakes to Avoid

1. **AIS vs Form 16 mismatch** — resolve before applying; lenders flag and escalate discrepancies
2. **Old or expired address proof** — utility bills must be within the past 3 months
3. **Non-RERA projects** — banks reject loan applications for unregistered under-construction properties
4. **Missing seller chain** — property title documents with gaps trigger legal delays
