## Assam Land Records in 2026: Full Digitisation via Dharitree

The Assam Revenue Department's Integrated Land Records Management System (ILRMS) — known as the **Dharitree portal** — has become the definitive online system for accessing land records in Assam. As of April 2026, it covers **all 35 districts of Assam** and allows citizens to view, search, and download land records without visiting any government office.

---

## What are Assam Land Records?

Assam land records (also called ROR — Record of Rights) document land ownership, classification, area, land use, and rights of occupancy for every parcel in the state. They include:

- **Jamabandi** — the register of rights showing owner name, dag number, area, and rights
- **Mutation register** — records ownership changes
- **Survey maps** — cadastral maps showing plot boundaries

---

## How to Access Assam Land Records Online — April 2026

**Step 1:** Visit the Dharitree portal at revenueassam.nic.in or dharitree.assam.gov.in

**Step 2:** Select 'View Land Records' or 'Jamabandi'

**Step 3:** Choose district, revenue circle, mouza (village), then enter the **Dag Number** (plot number) or owner name

**Step 4:** View and download the Jamabandi — which shows owner name, area in bighas/hectares, land classification, and nature of rights

**Step 5:** For mutation status, use the 'Mutation' section — enter mutation case number or application ID

---

## Mutation in Assam: Process and Timeline

Mutation (named **dakhil kharij** in Assam) is required whenever property ownership changes — through sale, inheritance, gift, or court decree.

**The process in 2026:**
1. File mutation application at the Circle Office (online via Dharitree in most districts)
2. Submit supporting documents (registered sale deed, death certificate for inheritance, etc.)
3. Revenue Circle Officer approves after verification
4. **Timeline:** typically 30–60 days

---

## Importance for Home Loan Applicants in Assam

Banks and HFCs require an **updated, mutation-complete land record** before disbursing home loans for properties in Assam. The Jamabandi must show the applicant as the registered owner.

If mutation has not been completed after purchase, the loan application will be stalled.

> **Obtain a fresh EC (Encumbrance Certificate) and updated Jamabandi before applying for a home loan in Assam.**
