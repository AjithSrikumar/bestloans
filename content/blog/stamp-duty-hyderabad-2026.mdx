## Stamp Duty in Hyderabad 2026: Quick Overview

Stamp duty and registration charges in Hyderabad / Telangana are levied on all property purchases under the Registration Act and Indian Stamp Act. They add approximately **6% to the property's market value** and must be paid from personal funds (not home loan amount).

---

## Current Stamp Duty Rates — Hyderabad April 2026

| Charge | Rate | Applicability |
|--------|------|--------------|
| **Stamp Duty** | **4%** of market value or government circle rate (whichever is higher) | All transactions |
| **Transfer Duty** | **1.5%** of market value | Resale/transfer transactions |
| **Registration Charges** | **0.5%** of market value | All transactions |
| **Total Effective Rate** | **~6%** | Resale transactions |

> For new construction: stamp duty and registration applies on the **undivided share of land (UDS)** at agreement stage; full registration at possession.

---

## Stamp Duty Calculation — Worked Examples

**Flat purchase in Hyderabad at Rs. 60 lakh (resale):**

| Component | Rate | Amount |
|-----------|------|--------|
| Stamp Duty | 4% | Rs. 2,40,000 |
| Transfer Duty | 1.5% | Rs. 90,000 |
| Registration | 0.5% | Rs. 30,000 |
| **Total** | **6%** | **Rs. 3,60,000** |

This Rs. 3.6 lakh must be arranged as personal funds — it **cannot be added to the home loan**.

## Who Gets a Concession in Hyderabad 2026?

- **Women buyers:** check with the local IGRS office — Telangana has discussed but not uniformly implemented women's stamp duty concession as of 2026
- **PMAY beneficiaries:** eligible buyers under the PMAY affordable housing scheme may receive exemptions — verify at time of registration
- **Agricultural-to-residential conversion:** different stamp duty rates may apply — verify with the sub-registrar

---

## Online Stamp Duty Payment — IGRS Telangana

1. Visit **igrs.telangana.gov.in** — the official IGRS portal
2. Use the e-Challan system to pay stamp duty online via NEFT, net banking, or at authorised bank branches
3. Receive e-challan as proof of payment; take this to the sub-registrar at registration appointment
4. Book registration appointment online at igrs.telangana.gov.in → 'Schedule Registration'

---

## The Hyderabad Registration Process — April 2026

Registration typically takes **1–2 hours** if all documents are ready. Bring:
- e-Challan for stamp duty payment
- Identity proofs for buyer and seller (Aadhaar + PAN)
- Two witnesses with ID proofs
- Photographs
- Original sale agreement

After biometric verification, the registered document is returned on the same day in digital form. Physical document follows within 7–10 days.
