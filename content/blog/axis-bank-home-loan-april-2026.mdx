## Axis Bank Home Loan — April 2026 Quick Facts

Axis Bank is one of India's top three private sector banks and offers one of the widest ranges of home loan product variants. In April 2026, Axis Bank home loan rates start at **7.60% p.a.** for floating-rate loans and are EBLR-linked, with quarterly rate resets (on 25th March, June, September, December). Axis passes on repo rate changes to borrowers within the same quarter.

## Axis Bank Home Loan Eligibility — April 2026

- **Age:** Minimum 21 years; maximum 65 years at loan maturity
- **Employment:** Salaried — minimum 2 years experience; self-employed — minimum 3 years business continuity
- **Loan amount:** Minimum Rs. 30 lakh; maximum up to Rs. 5 crore for standard schemes
- **CIBIL:** 700+ for approval; 750+ for best rate slabs

## Axis vs SBI vs HDFC — Which is Best for You in April 2026?

**Choose Axis Bank if:** You want a feature-rich loan with EMI holidays (Shubh Aarambh), OD facility (Super Saver), or step-down EMI (QuikPay). Also strong for builder tie-ups in premium projects.

**Choose SBI if:** You want the absolute lowest rate (7.25%) and value public sector stability and government employee benefits.

**Choose HDFC if:** You want fast processing, strong resale property expertise, and access to premium builder inventory.
