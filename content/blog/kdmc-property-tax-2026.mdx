## What is KDMC and Why This Tax Matters

The **Kalyan-Dombivli Municipal Corporation (KDMC)** is the civic body for the twin cities of Kalyan and Dombivli in Thane district, Maharashtra. Property tax is KDMC's primary revenue source funding roads, water supply, sanitation, parks, and emergency services.

In 2026, KDMC has significantly expanded its online payment capabilities — making compliance far simpler.

---

## KDMC Property Tax Calculation — 2026

KDMC calculates property tax based on **Annual Rateable Value (ARV)**, which depends on:

| Factor | Details |
|--------|---------|
| Property type | Residential/commercial/industrial |
| Built-up area | Square footage determines base value |
| Location (zone/ward) | Higher zones attract higher rates |
| Age of structure | Older buildings get depreciation |
| Occupancy status | Owner-occupied vs rented |

For residential properties:
- ARV = typically 1%–2% of market value per year
- Tax rate applied on ARV: **20%–30%** depending on property category and zone

## Key Deadlines and Rebates — FY 2026–27

| Deadline / Event | Details |
|------------------|---------|
| Annual tax due date | 31 March of each financial year |
| Early payment rebate | 5% rebate for payment in first quarter (April–June 2026) |
| Late payment penalty | 5%–20% interest per annum on overdue amounts |
| Abhaya Yojana | KDMC periodically introduces waiver schemes for long-overdue taxpayers — check the portal for active waivers in 2026 |

## How to Pay KDMC Property Tax Online — 2026

**Step 1:** Visit the official KDMC portal at **kdmc.gov.in** → Property Tax section

**Step 2:** Search your property using Property ID, owner name, or zone number

**Step 3:** View the outstanding tax bill and verify the amount and period

**Step 4:** Pay via net banking, debit card, credit card, or UPI

**Step 5:** Download and save the payment receipt — keep both digital and hard copies

## KDMC Property Tax and Home Loans

Banks lending against properties in the Kalyan-Dombivli jurisdiction require **current KDMC property tax receipts** as part of due diligence. Outstanding tax creates a charge on the property — which can block sale, transfer, or mortgage.

> **Ensure property tax is fully paid and up-to-date before initiating any home loan, balance transfer, or property sale transaction.**
