## Stamp Duty in Pune — April 2026 Current Rates

Pune falls under the Maharashtra Stamp Act, with uniform rates across Maharashtra.

| Buyer Type | Stamp Duty | Metro Cess | Registration | **Total** |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|-------------|---------|
| Male buyer | 5% | 1% | 1% (capped Rs. 30,000) | **~7%** |
| Female buyer | 4% | 1% | 1% (capped Rs. 30,000) | **~6%** |
| Joint (male + female) | 5% | 1% | 1% (capped Rs. 30,000) | **~7%** |

---

## How Ready Reckoner Rates Work in Pune

Stamp duty is calculated on the **higher of:**
- (a) The actual transaction (sale) price, or
- (b) The **Ready Reckoner (RR) rate** — the government's minimum assessed value for properties in that area

RR rates are published annually by the Maharashtra government and vary by zone, building age, and property type.

> Always check the applicable RR rate before signing any agreement — undervaluing the transaction below RR rate is illegal and will be corrected by the sub-registrar.

## Stamp Duty Calculation — Pune Example

**2BHK flat in Kharadi, Pune at Rs. 75 lakh (male buyer). RR rate value = Rs. 72 lakh.**

Since actual price (Rs. 75 lakh) > RR value, stamp duty applies on Rs. 75 lakh:

| Component | Rate | Amount |
|-----------|------|--------|
| Stamp Duty | 5% | Rs. 3,75,000 |
| Metro Cess | 1% | Rs. 75,000 |
| Registration | 1% (capped) | Rs. 30,000 |
| **Total** | | **Rs. 4,80,000** |

This must come from **personal funds**.

## Concessions Available in Pune April 2026

- **Female buyer:** 1% stamp duty concession (4% vs 5%) — save **Rs. 75,000** on the above example
- **PMAY-linked affordable housing:** stamp duty exemptions may apply — verify with IGR Maharashtra
- **Senior citizens:** no universal concession in Maharashtra as of April 2026 — check for special announcements

## Online Stamp Duty Payment — eSBTR Method

**Step 1:** Visit **igrmaharashtra.gov.in** → 'Pay Stamp Duty' → select 'eSBTR'

**Step 2:** Enter property details, transaction value, and buyer/seller information

**Step 3:** Pay online via net banking, debit card, or UPI → receive e-challan

**Step 4:** Book registration appointment at sub-registrar office online

**Step 5:** Visit with e-challan, identity proofs, two witnesses, and the draft sale deed — complete biometric registration

**Step 6:** Receive registered document digitally on the same day; physical copy in 7–10 days

## Stamp Duty and Your Home Loan Budget

Stamp duty and registration in Pune add **6%–7%** to the total property cost — on Rs. 75 lakh that is Rs. 4.5–5.25 lakh paid from personal funds. Plan for this alongside your down payment.

**The silver lining:** the stamp duty paid qualifies for **Section 80C deduction** in the year of payment (within the Rs. 1.5 lakh overall limit).

> Bank disbursement of your home loan will happen only after the **registered sale deed is submitted to the lender** — complete registration before expecting loan disbursal.
