## Why Tracking Your Home Loan Status Matters

The home loan journey from application to disbursement involves multiple stages — each with different timelines. Knowing exactly which stage your application is at helps you:

- Respond quickly to document requests
- Follow up on delays
- Anticipate when funds will reach the seller

In April 2026, all major lenders offer **digital tracking — no branch visits needed.**

---

## SBI Home Loan Status Check — April 2026

| Method | How to Use |
|--------|-----------|
| **Online** | Visit sbi.co.in → Personal Banking → Home Loans → Track Your Application. Enter Application Reference Number (ARN) and registered mobile |
| **YONO App** | Login → Loans → Home Loan → Application Status |
| **SMS** | Send `HLSTATUS <ARN>` to 9223488888 |
| **Helpline** | 1800-11-2018 (toll free) |
| **Branch** | Visit any SBI Home Loan Processing Centre with your ARN |

## What the Status Stages Mean

| Stage | What's Happening | Typical Timeline |
|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| **Application Received** | Form submitted; credit bureau check running | 1–2 days |
| **Document Verification** | Income, KYC, property documents being reviewed | 3–7 days |
| **Legal Verification in Progress** | Empanelled advocate checking property title | 5–10 days |
| **Technical Valuation in Progress** | Bank's engineer visiting and valuing the property | 3–5 days |
| **Credit Underwriting** | Credit team assessing full profile — income, obligations, CIBIL, property | 3–5 days |
| **Sanctioned** | Loan approved; sanction letter being issued. Review carefully before signing | 1–2 days |
| **Disbursement Requested** | Registered documents submitted; bank processing fund transfer | 2–5 days |
| **Disbursed** | Funds transferred to seller's account | Complete |

## What to Do If Your Application is Stuck

If your application shows no movement for **5+ working days** at any stage, escalate — don't wait:

1. Call the lender's home loan helpline
2. Email the processing center
3. Contact your relationship manager

If the issue persists beyond **10 working days** without explanation, file a complaint with the lender's Grievance Redressal Officer. For banks, the **Banking Ombudsman** is the final escalation if internal resolution fails.
