## Bank of Baroda Home Loan — April 2026 Snapshot

Bank of Baroda is India's third-largest public sector bank (by business) and one of the most competitive home loan lenders in April 2026. BOB's home loan rates start at **7.45% p.a.** — slightly above SBI's 7.25% but below most private banks. BOB has built a strong digital home loan platform and offers specific schemes for first-time buyers, women, and government employees.

---

## Bank of Baroda Home Loan Rates — April 2026

| CIBIL Score | Floating Rate (BRLLR-linked) |
|-------------|---------------------------|
| **750+** | 7.45%–8.00% p.a. |
| **700–749** | 8.00%–8.75% p.a. |
| **Women borrowers (750+)** | From 7.40% p.a. (0.05% concession) |

- **Processing fee:** 0.25%–0.50% of loan amount + GST
- **Zero processing fee** offered during festive campaigns and for certain government employee loans

## Bank of Baroda Home Loan Schemes — April 2026

| Scheme | Best For |
|--------|---------|
| **Baroda Home Loan** | Standard purchase, construction, renovation. Up to 30-year tenure, LTV up to 90% for loans up to Rs. 30 lakh |
| **Baroda Home Loan Advantage** | First-time buyer scheme with preferential rates and streamlined processing; digital application |
| **Baroda Vidyasthali** | For constructing homes near educational institutions in tier 2 cities |
| **Baroda Home Improvement Loan** | For renovation and extension of existing homes at competitive rates |
| **Baroda Pre-Approved Home Loan** | For existing BOB savings account holders — instant sanction based on account history |

## Bank of Baroda Home Loan Eligibility — April 2026

- **Age:** Minimum 21 years; maximum 70 years at loan maturity for self-employed (65 for salaried)
- **CIBIL score:** Minimum 650 (best rates for 750+)
- **Income:** No stated minimum; assessed on income-adequacy basis for EMI
- **Special advantage:** BOB is particularly strong for borrowers who have salary accounts with Bank of Baroda — pre-approved offers available with minimal documentation

## Bank of Baroda vs SBI — Which PSU Bank Offers Better Value?

| Factor | Bank of Baroda | SBI |
|--------|---------------|-----|
| **Best rate** | 7.45% | 7.25% |
| **Rate difference on Rs. 40 lakh, 20 yr** | ~Rs. 1.1 lakh more in total interest | Cheaper |
| **Digital platform** | Improved significantly in 2026 | Comparable for urban salaried |
| **Branch network** | ~8,500+ branches | ~22,000+ branches |

**Verdict:** For pure rate, SBI wins. For borrowers with existing BOB salary accounts or looking for pre-approved offers, BOB is a strong contender.

## How to Apply for BOB Home Loan Online — April 2026

1. Visit **bankofbaroda.in** → Loans → Home Loans → Apply Online
2. Enter income and property details for instant eligibility check
3. Submit digital application with uploaded documents
4. Track application status at bankofbaroda.in → Track Application
