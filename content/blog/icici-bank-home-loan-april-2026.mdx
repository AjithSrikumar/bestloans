## ICICI Home Loan Eligibility — April 2026

- **Age:** 21–65 years (loan must close before age 65)
- **Employment:** Salaried with minimum 2 years total experience; self-employed with 3+ years business continuity
- **CIBIL score:** 700+ for standard approval; 750+ for best rates
- **Minimum income:** Approximately Rs. 20,000/month net income
- **Special advantage:** Existing ICICI salary account holders get preferential rates and faster processing

## How to Apply for ICICI Bank Home Loan Online in 2026

1. Visit icicibank.com → Loans → Home Loan → Apply Now, or use the **iMobile Pay app**
2. Enter PAN and mobile number for instant pre-approval check (soft inquiry — no CIBIL impact)
3. Complete digital application with income and property details
4. Upload documents via the secure portal — no physical submission required
5. Video KYC and digital signing of sanction letter. Physical visit only for disbursement
