## DDA Housing Scheme 2026: Overview

The Delhi Development Authority (DDA) Housing Scheme 2026 offers residential flats in various categories at prices often below market rates in Delhi. DDA schemes are periodically launched and typically generate very high demand — oversubscription in popular categories is common. The scheme is open to **Indian citizens meeting specific eligibility criteria.**

---

## DDA 2026 Flat Categories and Approximate Prices

| Category | Description | Approximate Price Range |
|----------|-------------|------------------------|
| **EWS** (Economically Weaker Section) | Smaller units; priority for lower income households | Rs. 5–10 lakh |
| **LIG** (Low Income Group) | Eligibility based on income criteria | Rs. 12–25 lakh |
| **MIG** (Middle Income Group) | Standard flat sizes in outer Delhi areas | Rs. 30–60 lakh |
| **HIG** (High Income Group) | Larger flats in prime DDA-developed areas | Rs. 80 lakh–1.5 crore |

> **Note:** DDA prices and scheme details change with each launch. Visit **dda.delhi.gov.in** for the official current price list and scheme brochure.

---

## DDA Housing Scheme 2026 Eligibility

- Indian citizen of 18 years or above at the time of application
- Applicant or spouse must **not own any residential flat/plot** in Delhi, New Delhi, or Delhi Cantonment in their own name or jointly
- **Income criteria:** Varies by flat category (EWS requires income below Rs. 3 lakh annually; LIG below Rs. 6 lakh; MIG typically Rs. 6–18 lakh)
- For some categories: must **not have been allotted any flat** under any DDA scheme previously

---

## How to Apply for DDA Housing Scheme 2026 Online

1. Visit the official DDA portal at **dda.delhi.gov.in** → Housing → Current Schemes
2. Register using your Aadhaar-linked mobile number and PAN
3. Fill the application form, upload required documents (Aadhaar, PAN, income certificate, photograph)
4. Pay the registration/application fee online (varies by category — typically Rs. 1,000–25,000)
5. After the draw/allotment, successful applicants receive an allotment letter
6. Pay the initial deposit within the stipulated time to confirm allotment

## Getting a Home Loan for a DDA Flat in 2026

DDA flats are eligible for home loans from all major banks and HFCs. Processing is generally straightforward as DDA is a government authority with clear title deeds.

**Important:** Home loans for DDA flats are typically disbursed after possession — construction-linked disbursements are available for under-construction DDA projects.

- **SBI, HDFC, and Axis Bank** have specific DDA flat loan processing experience
- **PMAY subsidy** is available for eligible categories of DDA EWS/LIG flats
