## Form 16 in 2026: More Than Just a TDS Certificate

Form 16 has always been the primary income document for salaried home loan applicants. In 2026, its importance has increased further — banks now systematically **cross-check Form 16 data against the Annual Information Statement (AIS) and Form 26AS**.

Any mismatch between these three documents triggers additional scrutiny and can **delay loan sanction by weeks or even cause rejection**.

---

## What Form 16 Contains — and What Lenders Extract

| Form 16 Part | Content | What Lenders Use |
|-------------|---------|-----------------|
| **Part A** | Employer name, PAN, TAN; employee PAN; quarter-wise TDS | Verify employment continuity, employer legitimacy |
| **Part B** | Salary breakup (basic, HRA, allowances, perquisites); deductions claimed; net taxable income | Gross salary → loan eligibility; existing commitments |

**Lenders specifically extract:**
- Gross salary (to compute loan eligibility)
- Net taxable income
- Deductions declared (to understand existing obligations)
- TDS as proof of employment continuity
- Employer PAN/TAN to verify employer legitimacy

---

## The AIS Cross-Check: What Every 2026 Applicant Must Know

Since AY 2024–25, the AIS portal shows all income — salary, interest, dividends, capital gains, property transactions. Banks in 2026 increasingly request the AIS alongside Form 16.

**Scenario:** Form 16 shows a salary of Rs. 12 lakh, but AIS shows Rs. 15 lakh (because of a bonus not declared to the employer in time). The discrepancy will be flagged.

> **Action:** Download your AIS from incometax.gov.in and check for accuracy **at least 2 months before applying** for a home loan.

---

## When Form 16 is Not Available — Alternatives in 2026

| Situation | Alternative |
|-----------|------------|
| New employee (less than 1 year in current role) | Employer salary certificate + last 3–6 months salary slips + bank statements |
| Income below taxable limit | ITR acknowledgment with computation + salary slips + bank statements |
| Multiple employers in one year | Form 16 from each employer; if switching during year, submit current employer's salary certificate |

---

## Form 16 for AY 2026–27: Key Timeline

Employers are required by law to issue Form 16 for FY 2025–26 by **15 June 2026**.

If you are applying for a home loan before June 2026, lenders typically accept:
- Form 16 for FY 2024–25 **combined with** salary slips for the period April 2025–March 2026
