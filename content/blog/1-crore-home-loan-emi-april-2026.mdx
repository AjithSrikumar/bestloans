## Rs. 1 Crore Home Loan in April 2026: Key Facts

A Rs. 1 crore home loan is increasingly common in 2026 — driven by rising property prices in metro cities (average ticket size has reached Rs. 1.47 crore nationally). With the RBI repo rate at 5.25% and home loan rates starting at 7.10% p.a., the monthly EMI on a Rs. 1 crore loan at SBI's rate of 7.25% for 20 years is **Rs. 79,344** — significantly lower than the Rs. 95,000+ it would have been at 2022–23 rates.

## Minimum Salary Required for Rs. 1 Crore Home Loan

At 7.25% for 20 years (EMI = Rs. 79,344):

- With no existing EMIs, you need approximately **Rs. 1.45–1.75 lakh net monthly income** (FOIR 45–55%)
- At FOIR 40%: approximately **Rs. 1.98 lakh** net income required
- Adding a **co-applicant** with Rs. 60,000/month income reduces the solo income requirement to approximately Rs. 85,000–1.00 lakh
