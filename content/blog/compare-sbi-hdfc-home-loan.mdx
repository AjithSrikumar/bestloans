## SBI vs HDFC: Quick Overview

Both SBI and HDFC Bank are among India's most trusted home loan providers — together accounting for nearly 40% of all home loans disbursed. But which one is right for **you** depends on your profile, priorities, and timeline.

Let's compare them across every dimension that matters.

---

## Interest Rates

| Parameter | SBI | HDFC Bank |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| Starting Rate | **8.50%** p.a. | 8.75% p.a. |
| Maximum Rate | 9.85% p.a. | 9.65% p.a. |
| Rate Type | Floating (RLLR-linked) | Floating (RPLR-linked) |
| Rate Reset | Quarterly | Half-yearly |

**Winner: SBI** — offers a lower floor rate. Especially advantageous when the RBI cuts repo rates, as SBI transmits changes quarterly.

---

## Processing Fees

| Bank | Processing Fee |
|------|---------------|
| SBI | Nil to 0.35% + GST (often waived in promotions) |
| HDFC Bank | 0.50% or ₹3,000 minimum + GST |

**Winner: SBI** — frequently runs zero-processing-fee campaigns, especially during festive seasons.

---

## Loan Amount & Tenure

| Parameter | SBI | HDFC Bank |
|-----------|-----|-----------|
| Maximum Loan | No upper limit | No upper limit |
| Maximum Tenure | 30 years | 30 years |
| LTV Ratio | Up to 90% | Up to 90% |
| Minimum Loan | ₹5 Lakhs | ₹3 Lakhs |

Both banks offer equivalent loan amounts and tenure — **Tie**.

---

## Eligibility Criteria

| Criteria | SBI | HDFC Bank |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| Minimum Age | 18 years | 21 years |
| Maximum Age (maturity) | 70 years | 65 years |
| Minimum Income | ₹3 lakh/year | ₹10,000/month |
| CIBIL Score | 750+ preferred | 700+ preferred |

**Winner: SBI** — slightly more flexible on age, and accepts lower minimum CIBIL scores.

---

## Turnaround Time

| Metric | SBI | HDFC Bank |
|--------|-----|-----------|
| Sanction Letter | 5–7 working days | **3–5 working days** |
| Disbursement | 7–10 working days | **5–7 working days** |
| Digital Process | Partial (YONO app) | **Full end-to-end** |
| Dedicated Manager | Branch assigned | **Yes, dedicated RM** |

**Winner: HDFC** — significantly faster, fully digital, with a dedicated relationship manager.

---

## Unique Features

### SBI Advantages

- **SBI Maxgain** — overdraft facility on home loan (save on interest by parking surplus funds)
- **SBI Privilege Home Loan** — special rates for government/defence employees
- **Longer eligibility age** — up to 70 years at maturity
- **Larger PSU network** — strong presence in Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities

### HDFC Bank Advantages

- **HDFC Home Loan Assist** — fully digital journey from application to disbursement
- **Step-up EMI** — lower EMIs early on, increasing over time (great for young professionals)
- **Special rates for women borrowers** — 0.05% concession
- **Dedicated relationship manager** — one person handles your entire process

## When to Choose SBI

- You want the **absolute lowest interest rate**
- You're a **government employee** (Privilege or Defence schemes)
- You want the **SBI Maxgain overdraft** facility
- You're comfortable with a **slightly longer process**
- You're in a **Tier 2 or Tier 3 city** with strong SBI presence

## When to Choose HDFC Bank

- You need **fast disbursement** (e.g., builder deadline approaching)
- You prefer a **fully digital, paperless** experience
- You're a **first-time home buyer** wanting hand-holding
- You want a **dedicated relationship manager** throughout
- You're buying in a **metro or Tier 1 city**
