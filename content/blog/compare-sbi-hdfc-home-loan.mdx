## SBI vs HDFC Home Loan: Quick Comparison

Both SBI and HDFC are among India's most trusted home loan providers. But which one is better for you? Let's compare them on all key parameters.

## Interest Rates

| Parameter | SBI | HDFC |
|-----------|-----|------|
| Starting Rate | 8.50% p.a. | 8.75% p.a. |
| Maximum Rate | 9.85% p.a. | 9.65% p.a. |
| Rate Type | Floating (RLLR-linked) | Floating (RPLR-linked) |
| Reset Period | Quarterly | Half-yearly |

**Winner: SBI** – offers lower starting rates, ideal for those with excellent credit.

## Processing Fees

| Bank | Processing Fee |
|------|---------------|
| SBI | Nil to 0.35% + GST |
| HDFC | 0.50% or ₹3,000 min |

**Winner: SBI** – often waives processing fees during promotional periods.

## Loan Amount & Tenure

| Parameter | SBI | HDFC |
|-----------|-----|------|
| Maximum Loan | No upper limit | No upper limit |
| Maximum Tenure | 30 years | 30 years |
| LTV Ratio | Up to 90% | Up to 90% |

## Eligibility Criteria

### SBI Home Loan Eligibility:
- Minimum age: 18 years
- Maximum age at maturity: 70 years
- Minimum income: ₹3 lakh/year
- CIBIL Score: 750+ preferred

### HDFC Home Loan Eligibility:
- Minimum age: 21 years
- Maximum age at maturity: 65 years
- Minimum income: ₹10,000/month (salaried)
- CIBIL Score: 700+ preferred

## Turnaround Time

| Metric | SBI | HDFC |
|--------|-----|------|
| Sanction | 5–7 days | 3–5 days |
| Disbursement | 7–10 days | 5–7 days |
| Digital Process | Partial | Full |

**Winner: HDFC** – significantly faster processing with a fully digital journey.

## Special Features

### SBI Advantages:
- SBI Maxgain (overdraft facility on home loan)
- Tribal Plus Home Loan scheme
- Stamp duty included in loan amount
- NRI home loan options

### HDFC Advantages:
- HDFC Home Loan Assist (full digital journey)
- Step-up EMI option for young borrowers
- Special rates for women borrowers
- Dedicated relationship manager

## When to Choose SBI

Choose SBI if:
- You want the **lowest possible interest rate**
- You're comfortable with **slightly longer processing times**
- You want the **Maxgain overdraft facility**
- You already have a banking relationship with SBI

## When to Choose HDFC

Choose HDFC if:
- You need **fast loan disbursement**
- You prefer a **fully digital process**
- You're a **first-time home buyer**
- You want a **dedicated relationship manager**
